A rolling window - always the last 24 hours from right now, not tied to your local midnight. "Encounters" is every catch, raid catch, and fled Pokemon combined - the number that counts against Pokemon Go's rolling encounter limit. Raids are also included in that total; shown separately here just to break out how many of it were raids.

Privacy

When enabled, trainer names are hidden from all entries in the interface (and left blank in CSV exports). This only affects what's displayed - the name is still stored in the database.

Notifications

Off by default. Your browser will ask for permission the first time you turn one of these on.

Dashboard

Controls how far back the Catch Density Heatmap looks. Kept separate from the chart range above since the heatmap accumulates density over time and can turn into an undifferentiated blob if it spans too long a period.

Controls what counts as "today" and how the calendar/charts group catches by day. Defaults to your browser's timezone.

About PoGo Stats

A personal Discord bot + dashboard for tracking Pokemon Go catch and raid stats from PolygonX webhook messages. Built for personal, non-commercial use.

Credits

  • discord.py - the Discord bot library

  • Chart.js - the dashboard and raid charts
  • Leaflet.js - the interactive maps

  • CARTO - the free dark map tiles
  • OpenStreetMap contributors - the underlying map data
  • PokeAPI/sprites - the Pokemon sprite images
